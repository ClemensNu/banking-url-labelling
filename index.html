What is this?
A NUST research project collecting public labels on domain variants — helping identify domains belonging to banks, e-commerce platforms, and any institution that holds client financial information.
What do I do?
Visit the domain, then answer: is it a banking institution or not?
Banking includes
Banks, credit unions, mobile money services, microfinance institutions, and e-commerce platforms (e.g. online shops, payment portals).
Not banking includes
News, social media, government (non-financial), or anything else.
